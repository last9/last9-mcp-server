Change an alert rule's lifecycle or responder metadata without recreating it. Use is_disabled=false to promote/enable a validated rule, is_disabled=true to pause it, mute_until to mute or unmute it, and properties to replace its description, runbook, annotations, or query_mode. At least one change is required. This endpoint returns no replacement ID because PATCH preserves the existing rule ID. An entity-level snooze can prevent rule-level mute changes.
